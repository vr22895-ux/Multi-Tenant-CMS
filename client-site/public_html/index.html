about us

Metal Ministry Inc. is a Leading Manufacturer & Exporter of Ferrous & Non Ferrous Metals. Metal Ministry Inc. was incorporated by Mr. Dinesh Chandan and from then onwards a never-ending journey started to reach, satisfy and keep long lasting relationship with our old as well as new clients.
Metal Ministry Inc. is a proactive organisation engaged in supplying of quality products that meet and exceed customers' specific requirements and thus ensure complete satisfaction. We are primarily engaged in Ferrous & Non Ferrous Metals.
