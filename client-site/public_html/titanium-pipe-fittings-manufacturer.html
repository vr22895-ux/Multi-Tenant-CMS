Metal Ministry Inc. are manufacturers & suppliers of Titanium Pipe Fittings. Titanium Pipe Fittings are specially developed for piping requirements of industries like oil, gas, food processing, petrochemical, pharmaceutical and various others. These fittings offer superb piping solutions to the industries ensuring obstruction flow of materials such as oil, water, steam, air and gas without any risk of leakage. To make compatible with different industrial piping systems, these fittings are provided in different sizes, types, shapes and connection ends. The fittings are sturdily designated by utilizing superlative grade titanium in compliance with the ASTM, ANSI, DIN, JIS, EN and ISO quality standards.
